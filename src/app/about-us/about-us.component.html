About Us

Vision

Founded after an increase in hate crimes in our community. The Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol is a volunteer-based nonprofit committed to the safety and security of our community. We achieve this goal through our presence and services tailored to the needs of the City Line and Ozone Park Community. We are here to serve the community and its needs.

Who are we


The Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol was founded in early 2020 after rising hate crimes and overall crime in the City Line and Ozone Park community. We are a volunteer-based organization. Our volunteers undergo extensive training to handle all types of situations as well as obtaining a security license. We work side by side with the Ozone Park Block Association for the betterment of our community. We also maintain a good relationship with our three local precincts. Our volunteers patrol, observe and report daily as well as handling various situations.
