About Us

Vision

Founded after an increase in hate crimes in our community. The Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol is a volunteer-based nonprofit committed to the safety and security of our community. We achieve this goal through our presence and services tailored to the needs of the City Line and Ozone Park Community. We are here to serve the community and its needs.


The Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol was founded in early 2020 after rising hate crimes and overall crime in the City Line and Ozone Park community. We are a volunteer-based organization. Our volunteers undergo extensive training to handle all types of situations as well as obtain a security license. We maintain a good relationship with our three local precincts. Our volunteers patrol, observe and report daily, handle various situations and serve our community in many ways.

The mission of the Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol is to provide safety and security to the community through our presence and services tailored to the needs of the City Line and Ozone Park Community. The safety of the community is our priority. We are here to serve the community and its needs. We believe no unity without community

As a nonprofit organization Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol, and as individuals, we value above all else honesty, integrity, unselfishness, professionalism, and mutual respect. For our patrolmen, we offer a rewarding and challenging environment where personal development can flourish. We hold ourselves accountable to our community and patrolmen by honoring our commitments, providing results, and continually striving to provide the highest quality of civilian patrol.

We are committed to the ongoing improvement of the services we provide to the community. By investing in and developing our most important assets, our patrolmen, we aim to achieve all our goals and exceed the expectations of our community. Through our commitment to high standards, our vision is to earn the trust of our community by delivering the best services within the City Line and Ozone Park Community.
