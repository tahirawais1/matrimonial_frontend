Vision

Founded after an increase in hate crimes in our community. The Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol is a volunteer-based nonprofit committed to the safety and security of our community. We achieve this goal through our presence and services tailored to the needs of the City Line and Ozone Park Community. We are here to serve the community and its needs.
