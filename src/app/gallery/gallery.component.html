Our Services

Safety Patrol - Our volunteers regularly patrol City Line & Ozone Park. Our goal is to deter crime through the presence of our vehicle and the observation skills of our volunteers. We are the eyes and ears of the community. Our volunteers are trained to report emergencies in progress and undergo training to handle various incidents. Some of this training includes but isn't limited to CPR certification, crowd control training, and security certification. We coordinate and work with our local NYPD precincts to ensure the safety of the community.

Event Security


We work with community organizations, houses of worship, local sports teams, and local businesses to provide security at community events. The organizers can contact our organization by emailing info@copcp.org or calling (646) 851-0885. This service is free of charge.

Graffiti Removal


Copcp helps remove graffiti throughout the neighborhood through donated paint from residents and local businesses. We also report graffiti that our safety patrol observes to the local precincts.

Food Pantry


For the past year and a half, the Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol has run and operated the Ozone Park Food Pantry. Every Saturday, our volunteers distribute over $100 worth of fresh produce, dry goods, and canned goods to our community's over 800 families in need. The Food Pantry is held every Saturday at 83rd Street and Rockaway Blvd. We also host Pop-up pantries on select dates throughout the community to ensure we reach families in need.

Quality of Life Complaints


We assist residents with filing quality of life complaints and having them resolved. We work with the city agencies and police precincts to ensure complaints are resolved. To report abandoned vehicles and other quality of life issues, use our complaint form.

Snow Removal
